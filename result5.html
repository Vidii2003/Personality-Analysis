Type 9


YOUR NATURE

Your natural inclination.

⇓
  • You tend to value harmony and peace.
  • Being eager to cooperate with everything, you calmly make effort to keep the harmony between members of a group.
  • As a gentle-hearted and trusty person, you also try to avoid conflicts as much as you can.
  • This doesn't mean that you don't give your opinions, but you still maintain a cooperative manner.
  • You are often called a pacifist, or a meek person to others.

YOUR FEAR

We go on to overcome our inner fear.

⇓

As a person who values networking, you fear is mainly about losing connections between people, that is, being alone. You wish to live peacefully with others.
